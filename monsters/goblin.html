Goblin Warrior

Scrawny, spiteful, numble, and intelligent, a Goblin's insticnt for self preservation should never be underestimated. Though cowardly, Goblins readily band together if this secures an overwhelming advantage in numbers. Goblins frequently join with Orc armies (not always by choice) opportunistically helping themeselves to the spoils of war while others do most of the actual fighting.

Animosity

The goblin must attempt a Wp test when faced with heroic opponents. If the test fails then the goblin must immedietly attack one of the heroes. This is overpowerd by the goblins fear of elves.

Fear of Elves

The goblin must attempt a Wp test when faced with an elvish adversary. If the test fails then the goblin suffers -1 SL on all tests against them. The goblin may not move closer without passing another Wp test. If the elf moves closer to the goblin during this effect then another Wp test is needed to avoid the goblin becoming broken.

Night Vision

With the faintest source of light, the goblin can see into the darkness. The effective range of any light sources are extended 20 yards no matter how meager the source of light is.
